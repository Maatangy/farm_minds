Materials Needed

  • Fish tank
  • Water pump
  • Grow bed
  • Hydroton or other growing medium
  • Fish
  • Plants

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Setup the Fish Tank

Fill the tank with water and install the water pump. Make sure to cycle the tank for at least 24 hours before adding fish.

Step 2: Install the Grow Bed

Place the grow bed on top of the fish tank and fill it with the growing medium.

Step 3: Add the Plants

Plant your chosen vegetables or herbs into the grow bed.

Step 4: Add the Fish

Introduce the fish into the tank after ensuring the water conditions are suitable.

Maintenance Tips

Regularly check the water quality and maintain the pump. Ensure that the plants and fish are healthy and thriving.
